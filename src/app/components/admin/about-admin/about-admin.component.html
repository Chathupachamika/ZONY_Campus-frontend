OUR QUALITY POLICY

The Quality Policy of National Institute of Business Management is as follows. We at National Institute of Business Management seek to delight our customers by offering highest quality Degree, Higher Diploma, Diploma and Certificate courses, and ensure that we meet and exceed customer specific requirements and expectations.

In order to accomplish this we maintain a quality management system conforming to ISO 9001:2015 international standards.

We are committed to:

  • Excell in the provision of our products & services
  • Meet speed and quality of services at the most competitive cost
  • Continually innovate and improve our services

To achieve the above-mentioned objectives, we will:

  • Set and deploy SMART objectives to identify and improve our Marketing, Customer Management, Enrollments, Course Design and Development, Academic Support, Delivery, Library, Evaluations and Awarding Processes benchmarked to global standards.
  • Design an organizational structure in accordance with state-of-the-art technology.
  • Align ourselves with the best management Consultants available in the industry.
  • Design and implement well-structured training programs for all our academic / non-academic staff.
  • Instill an organizational culture that continually reacts to all risks and opportunities arising in the internal and external contexts and among our key stakeholders.
  • Maintain and improve the effectiveness of our Quality Management System (CMS) Continually.
